Dreamy Hot Chocolate

Prep Time:2 mins

Cook Time:30 mins

Total Time: 32 mins

Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients








Steps

  1. In a large stock pot over medium heat, add all of the ingredients.

  2. Heat and stir occasionally until the chocolate begins to melt and then turn to medium low and continue to stir every now and again until completely chocolatey and melted.

  3. Serve with whipped cream, marshmallows and sprinkles!

Notes

  • Store left overs in the refrigerator.
