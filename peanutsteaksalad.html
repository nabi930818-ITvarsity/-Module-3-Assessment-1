Thai Peanut Steak Salad

Prep Time:1 hour 15 mins

Cook Time:15 mins

Total Time: 1 hour 30 mins

Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients














Thai Peanut Sauce












Ginger Dressing










Steps

  1. Whisk together the peanut sauce ingredients and pour half into a gallon sized freezer bag.

  2. Add the steak and toss to coat. Place in the refrigerator for 1 hour or up to 2 hours.

  3. Prepare the Ginger Dressing, whisk in about 2 tablespoons of the peanut sauce, or to taste and store in the fridge until you're ready to eat.

  4. Place the steak in the freezer while you prep the salad, for up to 15-20 minutes.

  5. Prepare the veggies and greens in a large salad bowl or platter.

  6. Get the meat out of the freezer and slice thin, against the grain.

  7. Heat a skillet over medium high heat with a small drizzle of oil.

  8. Add the meat to the skillet and cook, tossing occasionally until cooked, about 3 minutes. Remove to a plate.

  9. Toss the meat with the veggies and top with dressing.

Notes

  • Left overs can be stored in the refrigerator for 2 days.
