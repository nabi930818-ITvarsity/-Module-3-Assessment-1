Garlic-Butter Parmesan Chicken With Lemon Rice

Prep Time:10 mins

Cook Time:40 mins

Total Time: 50 mins

Yield: 4 Servings

Ingredients










Rice








Steps

  1. In a pie dish or pan, combine the parmesan cheese, grated garlic, paprika, and Italian seasoning. Season the chicken with salt and pepper on each side; dredge in the parmesan mixture; shake off excess and set aside.

  2. In a large skillet that has a fitting lid, melt just 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat.

  3. Cook chicken until golden on each side and cooked through â€“ about 4-6 minutes for each side, depending on the thickness of your chicken. Transfer to a plate.

  4. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and 2 additional cloves of garlic. Quickly add the rice and stir continuously for 1 minute.

  5. Add the broth and lemon juice along with the salt and pepper and bring to a boil.

  6. Turn the heat to medium low and place the lid on the skillet. Cook until fluffy and no liquid remains, about 40-45 minutes.

  7. Remove from the heat and fluff with a fork.

  8. Add the chicken back in and cover with the lid. Place over the heat and re-warm the chicken.

  9. Serve with fresh parmesan and parsley.

Notes

  • You can keep this chicken and rice dish in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
