Balsamic Caprese Chicken

Prep Time:1 hour

Cook Time:14 mins

Total Time: 1 hour 14 mins

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients






Marinade










Balsamic Reduction



Steps

  1. In a ziploc bag, combine the oil, vinegar, garlic, basil, sugar, salt and pepper.

  2. Add the chicken, pressing out the air and sealing.

  3. Place in the refrigerator for 1 hour.

  4. Meanwhile, prepare the balsamic reduction

  5. In a high lipped skillet, over medium high heat, add the vinegar and sugar. Bring to a boil and then turn down to a simmer.

  6. Simmer for 10-15 minutes and remove from the heat to cool and thicken.

  7. Remove the chicken from the fridge.

  8. Heat a grill to medium high heat or heat the oven to 350.

  9. Place the chicken on the grill and turn down to medium heat. Cook for 6-7 minutes per side.

  10. In the oven method, merely put the chicken in a pan and cook for 30 minutes.

  11. Remove the chicken and top with two slices of tomato per breast and mozzarella cheese.

  12. Place under a broiler until melted.

  13. Remove the chicken to a place and drizzle with the balsamic reduction and sprinkle with basil, as well as a crack of black pepper if desired.

Notes

  • Left overs should be kept in the refrigerator.
